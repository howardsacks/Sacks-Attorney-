Frequently asked questions

How long does a property transfer take in Johannesburg?

Timeframes depend on documents, bond approvals and Deeds Office processing. We outline milestones early and keep you updated as each step is completed.

What documents do I need for conveyancing?

Typically ID, proof of address, marital status details and signed mandates. If a bond is involved, bank documents are also required.

Do you handle bond cancellations and registrations?

Yes. We assist with bond cancellation instructions and coordinate registration requirements with the relevant bank.

Can you help with sectional title transfers?

Yes. We guide compliance items and transfer documentation for sectional title properties.
